Top three things to see in Amsterdam

Sebastien Goddijn

Last updated Apr 7, 2017

1.

Barney's Coffeeshop

This is my favourite coffeeshop in the whole city. It is very close to my house and as such has provided countless good experiences for my friends and I. If you are ever in Amsterdam I highly reccommend going there, they have friendly service, incredible products, and an awesome menu for food and snacks that make it an unbeatable experience in my eyes.

Recommendation: Tangerine Dream.

2.

The Foodhallen

If you are looking for a nice lunch or dinner experience in Amsterdam the Foodhallen is the place to go. It has an open market style experience, with different bars selling different types of food and drink, ranging from sushi to french cheese and craft beer. It is an incredible environment to spend time in, a lot of people gather to meet with friends and have drinks in the evening, and this would be one of the highest recommendations I have for food in the entire city.

Recommendation: The Gin and Tonic bar is unbeatable.

3.

The Vondelpark

An amazing place to take your family or your friends. This is a beautiful area of the city, big open spaces and always a lovely atmosphere. On a sunny day, biking through this area is one of the highlights of any trip, and of course, bringing your own food and settling down for a picnic is highly reccomended. If you can, try and go on the weekend where you will see all of the locals out and about with music playing and good vibes all around.

Recommendation: Vanilla Bean Latte
